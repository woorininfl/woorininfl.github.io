Screencasting

This page is to present screencasting

Project 7. In this project I learned how to demonstrate software effectively using Camtasia.

I outlined the various steps for V-lookup function in EXCEL and recorded a narrated screencast that illustrates how to accomplish V-lookup.

Screencasts are usually used to deliver instruction and content in an efficient and effective manner. In this Screencast project, I explored how to create narrated screencasts for instructional tasks by creating a digital narrated screencast that walks us through a piece of software. I explained "v-lookup tool" in excel, where Camtasia was used. Zooming out, Smart focus, Mouse with highlight was employed to better explain the idea.
